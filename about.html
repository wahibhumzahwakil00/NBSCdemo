Bhagat Singh - A Revolutionary Hero

Born: September 28, 1907
Birthplace: Bangay, Punjab, British India
Died: March 23, 1931
Place of Death: Lahore Central Jail, British India

Bhagat Singh, born on September 28, 1907, in Bangay, Punjab, was a fearless freedom fighter and a key figure in the Indian independence movement. His unwavering commitment to the cause of a free India made him a symbol of courage and sacrifice.

From a young age, Bhagat Singh was deeply inspired by the sacrifices of earlier freedom fighters. His vision for a free India was one where every citizen enjoyed equal rights and opportunities, regardless of caste or creed.

Bhagat Singh's life was tragically cut short on March 23, 1931, when he was executed in Lahore Central Jail by the British colonial rulers. His sacrifice became a rallying cry for the independence movement, inspiring countless others to join the struggle for a free and sovereign India.

Legacy and Role Model

Bhagat Singh's legacy lives on as a symbol of fearlessness and patriotism. He continues to be a role model for generations, inspiring individuals to stand up against injustice and fight for the principles of freedom and equality.

Vision for Free India

Bhagat Singh envisioned a free India where every citizen had the right to live with dignity and enjoy the fruits of independence. His writings and speeches reflect a vision of a society free from exploitation and discrimination.

About Us

Welcome to NEW BHAGAT SINGH CLUB

Our mission is to empower and uplift the lives of underprivileged students through comprehensive education and support. At NBSC, we are dedicated to providing a transformative learning experience that goes beyond traditional classroom boundaries.

  1. Supporting the Underprivileged:

    We are committed to extending a helping hand to economically disadvantaged students. Through various programs and initiatives, we aim to create an inclusive learning environment where every student has the opportunity to thrive.

  2. Guiding and Mentoring:

    Our experienced mentors and educators are passionate about guiding students on their academic journey. We offer personalized mentorship to help students navigate their educational paths, make informed decisions, and overcome challenges.

  3. Quality Education:

    NBSC believes in the power of education to bring about positive change. We provide high-quality teaching and learning resources to ensure that every student receives a well-rounded education that prepares them for success not only in academics but also in life.

  4. College Readiness:

    We go beyond conventional teaching methods to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary for admission to esteemed colleges and universities. Our goal is to empower students to excel academically and fulfill their dreams of pursuing higher education.

  5. Preparation for Competitive Exams:

    Recognizing the importance of competitive exams such as JEE Mains, NEET, CETs, and UPSC, we offer specialized training programs. Our rigorous exam preparation strategies are designed to enhance students' confidence, knowledge, and performance in these competitive assessments.

At New Bhagat Singh Club, we believe in the potential of every student, regardless of their background. Join us on this educational journey, where we strive to make a lasting impact on the lives of aspiring individuals, guiding them towards a future filled with opportunities and success. Together, let's build a brighter tomorrow through education.
